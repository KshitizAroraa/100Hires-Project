You're wasting your time creating the wrong SEO content. According to Hrefs, 97% of content gets no traffic from Google. And AI hasn't made that any easier. One study showed that CTR drops by 70% when there's an AI answer in Google. So the question is, should you just stop creating SEO content altogether? Well, the answer is no. But you do need to change your strategy. Here's what you need to do instead. Number one, don't target keywords that AI can easily answer, like what is personal injury. Number two, create a highly relevant page targeting a commercial keyword like car accident lawyer in Chicago using a tool like Rankability. Number three, create at least 10 assets to support and internally link to this commercial page. And number four, which is most important, only spend 20% of your time creating content on your website. The other 80% should focus on driving reviews, getting relevant back links, and acquiring brand mentions on trusted websites. This is what will make the biggest difference in your search and AI visibility.
