# Algorithm update casualty analysis — AI citations mirror organic traffic losses

- **Author:** Lily Ray
- **URL:** https://lilyraynyc.substack.com/p/are-citations-in-ai-search-affected
- **Why this post:** Analyzed 11 sites hit by an unconfirmed January 2026 Google update using real Ahrefs traffic data; found AI-search citation declines tracked almost exactly with organic traffic declines — hard evidence that classic SEO visibility and AI visibility are still linked, not separate games.

---

Ray identified 11 subfolders that lost 5.7% to 53.1% of their organic traffic between January 20 and February 16, 2026. Using Ahrefs Brand Radar, she cross-referenced each subfolder's AI search citation counts across ChatGPT, AI Mode, Perplexity, and Gemini over the same window.

The pattern was consistent: traffic losses were mirrored by citation losses on nearly every platform, with ChatGPT showing the steepest declines — notable since ChatGPT isn't a Google product, yet reacted more sharply than Google's own Gemini. Perplexity was the outlier, often showing citation growth despite the traffic losses.
