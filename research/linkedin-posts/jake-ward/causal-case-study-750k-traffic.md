# Causal AI case study — 0 to 750K/mo SEO traffic

- **Author:** Jake Ward
- **URL:** https://learn.byword.ai/seo-strategy/case-study-causal
- **Why this post:** The single most-cited case study on this whole list.
- **Disclosure note:** Ward co-founded Byword, the AI content tool used to produce these results. Independent coverage (Single Grain) flagged that this wasn't always disclosed upfront in his original case study posts. The results appear real, but the framing is self-interested.

---

Ward documented taking a B2B SaaS/finance website from zero to 750,000 monthly organic visitors within 12 months using 100% AI-generated content produced via Byword, starting on GPT-3/GPT-3.5 and later moving to GPT-4. He described the approach as AI-generated content for broad, top-of-funnel topics combined with human-written content for more personal, bottom-funnel topics.
