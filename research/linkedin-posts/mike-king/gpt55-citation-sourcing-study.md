# GPT-5.5 citation sourcing study

- **Author:** Mike King
- **URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/michaelkingphilly
- **Why this post:** Highly technical, reverse-engineered study of how GPT-5.5 actually sources citations — the kind of "explains the mechanism, not just the outcome" research that distinguishes King from commentary-only voices.

---

King documented that GPT-5.5 exposes its search providers through parameters like `result_source` and ranking position via `ref_index`. He found roughly 80% of the products LLMs recommend trace directly back to search-engine ranking position — to get cited on business-related queries, a brand essentially needs a Bing top-10 spot.

He also tracked how the share of citations going to "best X in 2025/2026" style listicles jumped from ~5% under GPT-5.2 (January) to ~80% by May under GPT-5.5.
