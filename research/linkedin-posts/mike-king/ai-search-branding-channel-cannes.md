# AI search as a branding channel (Cannes Lions 2026)

- **Author:** Mike King
- **Why this post:** Direct quote-worthy framing on how AI search should be budgeted differently from performance channels — relevant strategic context for anyone producing AI-era SEO content.

---

Interviewed at Semrush Villa at Cannes Lions, King argued that AI search platforms function more as branding channels than performance channels, meaning marketers should expect different KPIs and expect to spend less time on classic performance-channel tactics as AI search advances.
