# 400 websites that survived the traffic collapse

- **Author:** Rand Fishkin
- **URL:** https://sparktoro.com/blog/5-strategic-features-that-predict-survival-in-the-zero-click-era/
- **Why this post:** Rare "what's working" angle rather than just documenting decline — amplifies a colleague's (Cyrus Shepard's) analysis and adds his own framing, showing collaborative practitioner research rather than solo takes.

---

Fishkin highlighted analysis by Cyrus Shepard of 400 websites that did NOT lose traffic during the 2024–2026 "traffic apocalypse," identifying five shared features that predicted survival. Fishkin's own takeaway: no amount of tactical SEO excellence saves a site whose underlying business model is one Google/AI can disintermediate — the fix has to be structural, not tactical.
