# Zero Click Marketing book announcement

- **Author:** Rand Fishkin
- **Why this post:** Ties together years of zero-click research into a concrete, actionable resource (book + workshops), and cites a specific, checkable figure (46% traffic decline over 3 years) rather than vague doom-saying.
- **Disclosure note:** Book co-authored with Amanda Natividad, published by Damn Gravity.

---

Fishkin and Amanda Natividad (SparkToro's Chief Evangelist) announced a book, *Zero Click Marketing: How to Build Brands and Earn Customers on a Traffic-Starved Web*, citing data showing open-web traffic has declined 46% over the past three years. The announcement post drew 620+ reactions and 89 comments from marketers discussing the same structural shift.
