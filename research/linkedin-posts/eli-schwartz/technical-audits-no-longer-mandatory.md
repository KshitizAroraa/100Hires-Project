# Technical SEO audits no longer the mandatory starting point

- **Author:** Eli Schwartz
- **URL:** https://substack.com/@productledseo
- **Why this post:** Direct pushback on an "SEO is obsolete" sales pitch some agencies use — shows independent, sometimes contrarian thinking rather than following industry consensus.

---

Schwartz noted a reversal in what he hears from prospective clients: early in his consulting practice, agencies insisted every SEO engagement must start with a technical audit; now some agencies push the opposite framing, using "SEO is obsolete" as a sales pitch. He pushes back on this oversimplification.
