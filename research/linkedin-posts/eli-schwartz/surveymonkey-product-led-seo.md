# SurveyMonkey product-led SEO case study (GTM 116 podcast)

- **Author:** Eli Schwartz
- **URL:** https://thegtmnewsletter.substack.com/p/gtm-116-the-future-of-seo-and-ais-8d1
- **Published:** 2024-10-08
- **Why this post:** Concrete, named track record across multiple companies (SurveyMonkey, Tinder, Coinbase) rather than a single case study — tests whether his framework holds across different business models.

---

Schwartz detailed how he took SurveyMonkey's organic search from near-zero to one of the company's largest growth channels using a product-led SEO approach, and discussed applying similar principles at Tinder and Coinbase. He argues AI content generation alone isn't a silver bullet — it still requires deliberate strategy.
