# Moz guide on AI visibility measurement

- **Author:** Aleyda Solis
- **Why this post:** Sparked a real technical discussion in the comments about "extractability" with a specific figure (24 percentage point citation gap) — shows engaged practitioner dialogue, not just one-way broadcasting.

---

Solis argued AI search doesn't replace SEO fundamentals (crawlability, indexability, entity consistency, authority) but changes how visibility should be prioritized and measured — moving beyond traffic as the main success proxy.

A commenter (from DerivateX) added that they'd measured a 24-percentage-point citation-rate gap between high- and low-"extractability" versions of identical content, controlling for retrieval rank.
