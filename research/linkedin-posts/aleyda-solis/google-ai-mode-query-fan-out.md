# Google AI Mode's query fan-out explainer

- **Author:** Aleyda Solis
- **URL:** https://www.aleydasolis.com/en/ai-search/google-query-fan-out/
- **Why this post:** Foundational explainer that other practitioners (including Marie Haynes, independently) built on — good evidence of being an original source rather than someone repeating others' frameworks.

---

Solis explained how Google AI Mode uses a "query fan-out" technique — issuing multiple related sub-queries concurrently across subtopics and data sources, then synthesizing the results into one response. She argued this shifts SEO from keyword-focused optimization to deeper intent understanding, comprehensive topical coverage, and anticipating follow-up questions.
