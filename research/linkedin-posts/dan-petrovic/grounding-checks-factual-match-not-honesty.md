# AI grounding doesn't check honesty, only factual matching

- **Author:** Dan Petrovic (DEJAN Marketing)
- **URL:** https://dejan.ai/blog/ai-spam/
- **Published:** 2026-02-18
- **Why this post:** Identifies a real, specific spam/manipulation vector in AI grounding systems — shows critical, self-aware analysis (he even flags his own agency's content as an example of the gray area) rather than purely promotional research.

---

Petrovic explained that AI grounding systems check whether a claim exists in a source document, not whether that source is honest — creating an exploitable gap where self-promotional "Best X Companies" pages can get cited as if they were neutral third-party rankings.

He candidly noted his own agency's similar page had been submitted to DEJAN's own spam-detection tool and accepted as a valid training example, acknowledging the same structural pattern applies to his own content.
