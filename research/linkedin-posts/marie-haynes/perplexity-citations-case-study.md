# Client case study — 0 to 10 Perplexity citations in 2 weeks

- **Author:** Marie Haynes
- **URL:** https://ca.linkedin.com/in/marie-haynes
- **Why this post:** A real, dated, specific client result — exactly the kind of checkable claim the task brief wants, not a general framework.

---

Haynes described a client project where they replaced three separate 2,000-word blog posts with a single in-house industry report. The result: the client went from 0 to roughly 10 citations on Perplexity within two weeks. She also noted that a lack of brand mentions on Reddit/niche forums was previously hurting trust in AI-generated recommendations for the client.
